Oats pancake with cocoa

This recipe is especially made for the people who go to gym or wanna eat with less calories

So you need the next few ingredients and these are :

  • 1 cup and a half of oats (obviosly)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 TBSP of nut butter of your choice
  • 1 TBSP of cocoa powder or protein of your choice
  • 1 banana

How to prepare

You have to put all the ingrediends above in a blender.That's all.

An then, after it's having a liquid form you have to put it in a pan and cook all the pancakes. The flavour is your choice

Voila! A very easy and very cheap recipe.
