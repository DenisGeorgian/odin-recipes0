HEALTY MOCHA

Sometimes you feel like you did not drink a latte in a long time but you are in your healty week

NO PROBLEMO!

INGREDIENTS THAT YOU NEED:

  • A cup with some ice
  • 1 cup cold brew
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 TBSP maple syrup
  • 1 TBSP of cocoa

How to prepare :

YOU need a different cup to mix all the ingredients(except the cup with ice). You have to put the cold brew int the empty cup with 1/2 cup milk, 1/2 TBSP maple syrup and 1 TBSP cocoa. Then mix all the ingredients. Then pour the mixed ingredients in the cup with ice

VOILA!

YOU made are really healty mocha
