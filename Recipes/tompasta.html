TOMATO PASTA

<h2>Tomato sauce pasta</h2>

Those tomato sauce pasta are one of my favorite pasta recipes

Here are the ingredients :

  • Some uncooked pasta
  • Tomato sace
  • 1 onion
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • One Tbs of chili
  • One Tbs of oregano
  • 1/4 Tbs of rosemary
  • 1 Tbs of basil
  • 50 ml of milk
  • cherry tomatos
  • Mozarella light

HOW TO COOK :

First you have to boil the pasta.Then you have to cut the onion and the garlic cloves in pieces and put them in the pan. After they onion and garlic are cooking add the chili, oregano, rosemary, and basil. Leave for around 3-4 minutes and then add the tomato sauce togheter with the milk in the pan and then you mix. After the sauce is starting boil, add the mozarella. After that mix the contains of the pan with the pasta and VOILA a really good tomato pasta recipe.
